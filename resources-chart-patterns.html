Chart patterns are formations that appear on price charts that may help predict future price movements. They represent the psychological battles between buyers and sellers and often signal continuation or reversal of existing trends. This library contains detailed information on the most reliable patterns seen in cryptocurrency markets.

Each pattern includes real cryptocurrency chart examples, statistical success rates based on historical data, optimal entry and exit strategies, and key levels to watch.

Reversal Patterns

Head and Shoulders

A bearish reversal pattern consisting of three peaks, with the middle peak (head) being higher than the two surrounding peaks (shoulders).

Bearish

Inverse Head and Shoulders

A bullish reversal pattern consisting of three troughs, with the middle trough (head) being lower than the two surrounding troughs (shoulders).

Bullish

Double Top

A bearish reversal pattern where price reaches a high point, retraces, and then reaches a similar high point before declining.

Double Bottom

A bullish reversal pattern where price reaches a low point, rebounds, and then reaches a similar low point before rising.
